About / Help

Welcome to Destined Encounters, where you can shape a romantic storyline through AI-generated narratives and interactions. This application blends storytelling, romance, and dynamic AI-driven characters to give you a unique, customizable experience.

Basic Usage

  1. Personalize Your Characters: On the Personalize page, fill out details for both yourself (the user) and the NPC. You can pick from dropdowns or type in your own custom entries.
  2. Set the Environment: Decide where the story begins—at a café, a gym, etc. Select from the provided options or type in a custom location.
  3. Begin Interaction: After saving your details, go to the Interaction page. Type in actions or dialogue to guide the story, and the AI will respond with a narrative and the NPC’s perspective.

Generating Images

  1. Auto-Generate Prompt: Click “Auto Generate Scene Prompt” to have the AI produce a single-sentence description of the current scene. You can then generate an AI image from that prompt.
  2. Customize for Better Results: If the auto-generated prompt is lacking detail, feel free to edit it before hitting “Generate Scene Image.” Consider adding words like “warm lighting” or “casual pose” to get more consistent results.
  3. One Image per Turn: By default, you can only generate an image once per turn to keep the story flow balanced.

Mid-Game Customizations

Sometimes you’ll need to adjust the NPC’s details (like changing hair color, clothing, or personality) or shift the environment. You can do this via the “Update NPC” section on the Interaction page. This is helpful if the story takes a new direction or if you need to clarify details that the AI has started to “forget.”

Affection & Relationship Stages

  1. Monitor Affection: The NPC’s affection score changes based on how they react to your actions.
  2. Stages of Relationship: As affection grows, the relationship moves through stages (Strangers → Casual → Comfortable → Close → Serious → Committed).
  3. Unlock Perks: Each stage unlocks new possibilities, from casual flirting to serious future-planning discussions.

OOC Commands

To give the AI special instructions, simply begin your action with “OOC:”. For example, OOC: Make the NPC act more shy about the future date. This is useful if you need to reshape the AI’s response or correct its memory without changing the official “Update NPC” fields.

Important Notes

  • Frequent Updates & Timeouts: We’re actively adding new features, and sometimes your session may time out while we deploy changes. We currently don’t have a way to save sessions, but we’re working on it.
  • Occasional Repeated Replies: The narrator may occasionally return the same reply. If that happens, just press “Send” again; it usually resolves on the second try (we’re still investigating why this occurs).

These features may be part of a future subscription model due to the higher costs involved in running them.
