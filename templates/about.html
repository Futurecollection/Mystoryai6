How It Works

1

Create Your Characters

Design both yourself and your AI companion with detailed traits including personality, appearance, and background. All characters must be 20+ years old.

2

Choose Your Setting

Set the stage for your encounter — select from various locations like cafés, parks, or offices, or create your own custom environment.

3

Shape Your Story

Drive the narrative through your choices and actions. Watch as your relationship evolves naturally based on your interactions.

4

Generate Visuals

Create images of scenes and characters using multiple AI image models including Flux, Pony, and Realistic Vision with customizable parameters.

5

Save & Continue

Your story progress is saved automatically when logged in, or continue as a guest without saving. Return anytime to continue your romantic journey.

Special Features

Interaction Modes

Choose how you want to experience your story:

  • Narrative Mode: Immersive third-person storytelling with rich descriptions
  • Dialogue Mode: Direct character conversations focused on dialogue and actions
  • OOC Commands: Use "OOC:" (Out of Character) to guide the AI without breaking immersion

Story Generation

Transform your interactions into immersive content:

  • Convert dialogue and actions into a polished erotic story
  • Process longer stories in chunks for seamless narrative flow
  • Add custom instructions to control the tone and style
  • Continue where you left off if the story gets cut off

Multiple Image Models

Choose from different AI image generation styles:

  • Portrait Specialist: Photorealistic portrait style images
  • Detailed Scene Creator: Customizable rendering with advanced parameters
  • Artistic Visualizer: High-quality artistic scene visualization
  • Control parameters like steps, scheduler type, and guidance scale
  • Save favorite images to your personal gallery

Relationship Stages

Experience evolving intimacy through 6 relationship stages:

  • Stage 1: Playful Connection & Getting to Know Each Other
  • Stage 2: Deepening Chemistry
  • Stage 3: Growing Emotional Intimacy
  • Stage 4: Romantic & Physical Connection
  • Stage 5: Mature Dating Phase
  • Stage 6: Committed Relationship
  • Customize stage descriptions to create your ideal progression

Character & Scene Management

Dynamic Character Development

  • Characters automatically update based on story progression
  • View character's private thoughts and evolving biography
  • Characters remember details from previous conversations
  • Manually edit character backstory and thoughts when needed
  • Mid-game character customization to adjust appearance or personality

Scene Control

  • Change location, time of day, and weather during your story
  • Adjust scene mood to match the emotional tone
  • Update environment details without restarting your story
  • Track relationship statistics including affection and trust
  • View and access your full conversation history
  • Gallery to save and manage generated images

Account Features

User Accounts

  • Register to save all your stories and characters
  • Automatic session saving when logged in
  • Continue from where you left off on any device
  • Start multiple stories with different characters

Guest Mode

  • Try the app instantly without registration
  • Full access to all features during your session
  • Create and interact with characters on the fly
  • Upgrade to an account later if you want to save progress

Important Notes

Age Restriction

All characters must be explicitly 20+ years old. Any content involving minors is strictly prohibited and will be blocked by our system.

Frequent Updates

We're actively improving the application with new features. Occasionally your session may time out during deployments.

Image Generation Limits

Each story allows up to 5 image generations to ensure API availability for all users. Save important images to your gallery.
